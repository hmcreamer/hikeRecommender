About

Ever since I started going on hikes as a young kid with my family, I have enjoyed exploring the outdoors by finding new trails to hike. I have a handful of favorite trails that I hike regularly, but I like to find new trails all of the time. During college, my friends and I discovered the art of "sending it". We simply looked for exciting, new trails that we could explore and then we "sent it" by hiking the trails through rain, snow, and sun. While we always had fun on these adventures, we inevitably enjoyed some of the trails much more than others. This got me thinking... what if there was a way to recommend hikes based on one's preferences and past experiences in order ensure that they can find the best possible trail to hike?

I have created this site in order to help answer this question. By taking users' preferences and finding similarities between trails, this site uses several recommendation algorithms in order to create a list of trails for a user that they will enjoy hiking. Hopefully this site will be helpful for people who want to "send it" like me and my friends by going on a hike to explore the outdoors.

Directions

In order to get started, simply click the 'Find a Hike' button and fill out the information on the page. There's very little time and a lot to explore out there (see below). So go "send it"!
